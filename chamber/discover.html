Come to discover Jeremie!

Discover the charm of Jérémie, Haiti — where golden beaches meet vibrant traditions. Taste authentic flavors like the famous pisket plate, explore historic forts, wander lively markets, and hike lush national parks. Join colorful February carnivals, honor Haitian pride on Flag Day, and feel the heartbeat of local culture. Whether you crave adventure, history, or relaxation, Jérémie invites you to create unforgettable memories in the Pearl of the Grand’Anse.
