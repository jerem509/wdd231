Membership Application

You're invited!

The Jeremie Chamber of Commerce warmly invites your application for membership. Our mission is to foster local economic growth, support business development, and build a strong, vibrant community where businesses and entrepreneurs thrive. As a member, you gain access to valuable opportunities for networking, business promotion, advocacy, and support within the Jeremie business community.

Whether you are a new entrepreneur or an established business leader, your membership helps strengthen our collective voice and supports the prosperity of Jeremie and the surrounding region. Thank you for considering joining us—we are excited to work together to support your success and the growth of our local economy.

Membership Criteria and Benefits

Full membership is open to any individual, business, organization, or company located in the Jeremie area or its surrounding regions that is involved in any trade, business, profession, or occupation. Financial members enjoy full voting rights, representation, and eligibility to serve on committees and the chamber’s board.

Members will be notified of events and updates through email, with additional publicity through local newspapers and radio stations.

By submitting this application, you consent to the Chamber publishing your business details in the public member directory on our website.
